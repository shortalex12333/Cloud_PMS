ENGINE MAINTENANCE MANUAL
==========================

CATERPILLAR C32 ACERT MARINE DIESEL ENGINE
Model: C32 ACERT
Serial Number: CAT32-2024-001

IMPORTANT SAFETY INFORMATION
-----------------------------
Always ensure proper ventilation when working on engines.
Wear appropriate personal protective equipment.
Follow all manufacturer guidelines.

SCHEDULED MAINTENANCE
--------------------

DAILY CHECKS:
1. Check engine oil level
2. Inspect for leaks
3. Check coolant level
4. Verify fuel filters
5. Test alarm systems

WEEKLY MAINTENANCE:
1. Check air filter condition
2. Inspect belts and hoses
3. Test battery voltage
4. Verify coolant quality
5. Check exhaust system

MONTHLY PROCEDURES:
1. Change engine oil and filters
2. Inspect turbocharger
3. Check injector performance
4. Test cooling system pressure
5. Verify fuel quality

ANNUAL SERVICE:
1. Complete engine inspection
2. Replace all filters
3. Test compression
4. Inspect valve clearances
5. Check governor settings

TROUBLESHOOTING
---------------

Problem: Engine won't start
- Check fuel supply
- Verify battery charge
- Inspect starter motor
- Check safety interlocks

Problem: Loss of power
- Inspect air filter
- Check fuel filters
- Verify turbocharger operation
- Test injectors

Problem: Overheating
- Check coolant level
- Inspect water pump
- Verify thermostat operation
- Check heat exchanger
